Finishing Options

How Do You Want It?

Every print comes raw from the printer. You choose what happens next.

Popular
🎨

Painted & Finished By Us

We hand-paint and finish your model with professional acrylic paints, primer, and sealant. Ready to display straight out of the box.

  • Primed, hand-painted, sealed
  • Colour matching from reference images
  • Weathering & detail effects available
  • Perfect for gifts or display pieces
  • Slightly longer turnaround time
🖌️

Raw Print — Paint It Yourself

Get the printed model cleaned, sanded smooth, and primed — ready for you to paint exactly how you want it. Great for hobbyists.

  • Supports removed, surface sanded
  • Primed and ready for paint
  • Faster turnaround
  • Full creative control for you
  • Lower cost option

How It Works

Simple and straightforward — from idea to finished print in a few days.

01

Tell Us What You Want

Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your model idea, reference images, and size requirements.

02

We Source the Model

We find the best 3D model from our professional libraries and send you a preview for approval.

03

Choose Your Finish

Decide if you want us to paint it or if you'd like to colour it yourself. We'll give you a price and timeline.

04

Print & Deliver

We print, finish, and arrange collection or local delivery. Your model ready to go.
